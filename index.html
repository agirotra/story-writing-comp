Three Categories

Choose Your Story World

How to Enter

Three Simple Steps

Participating is easy. Your story could be read around the world.

✍️

Write Your Story

Pick your category, match your age group, and write an original story within the word count. Save it as a Word Doc (.docx) or PDF. No AI tools, no plagiarism — your voice, your story.

📝

Fill the Form & Pay

Complete the registration form below with your details and pay ₹490 securely via Razorpay — UPI, cards, and net banking all accepted. You'll get a payment confirmation instantly.

🚀

Upload & Submit

Upload your story file (.docx or PDF) directly in the form. After payment, it's saved automatically — no email needed. Submit before 30th June 2026. Our literary jury will evaluate entries and announce results. Follow @bukmuklibrary for updates.

Important Rules

Before You Submit

Please read these guidelines carefully before entering.

✅ Your story must be 100% original work — written entirely by you.
🚫 Do not use AI tools (ChatGPT, etc.) to write or edit your story.
📏 Respect the word count for your age category — no more, no less.
👩‍⚖️ The jury's decision is final and binding for all entries.
🗂️ Submit your story as a Word document (.docx) or PDF only.
📅 All entries must be submitted by 30th June 2026.
